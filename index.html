📄 关于我 📄 About Me

你好，很高兴与你相逢于此。 Hello, nice to meet you here.

我是追风的人，一个偏爱安静、心怀温柔的普通人。 I am the Wind Chaser, an ordinary person who loves quietness and carries tenderness.

不喜喧嚣热闹，独爱岁月清宁，习惯在平淡的日子里收集世间所有美好。热爱视觉创作、喜欢打磨版式与画面，钟情于用审美装点生活，把细碎的温柔藏进每一处细节里。 Not keen on noise and bustle, I cherish tranquil days, collecting all the beauty in ordinary life. I love visual creation, refining layouts and images, decorating life with aesthetics, and hiding delicate tenderness in every detail.

心有山海，静而不争。不慌不忙，慢慢前行，相信世间所有奔赴，皆有归途，所有美好，终会如期而至。 With mountains and seas in heart, serene and unhurried. Take it easy and move forward, believing every journey has its destination, and all beauty will come as promised.

愿往后日子，追风赶月不回头，平安喜乐度余生，也愿路过此间的你，岁岁安然，万事顺遂。 May the days ahead be free of looking back, filled with peace and joy. And may you who pass by here be safe and sound, with everything going smoothly.
